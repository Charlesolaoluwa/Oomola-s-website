About  

Bardi Osobuanomola Catherine is from Delta State, Nigeria. She has a Bachelor’s degree from the University of Benin.

Bardi tells stories deeply rooted in the metaphysics of ancient and modern Africa as an indigenous bystander that focuses on superstitions, evocations, societal happenings and their close companions. She creates for self and never shies away from personal truths, revolting themes and situations.
